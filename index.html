 DIY vs done-for-you
    
Why not just use Squarespace or Wix?

    
Website builders are useful tools — but the tool isn’t the strategy. You can spend hours dragging sections around and still end up with a site that looks okay yet doesn’t explain your offer clearly, build trust, show up properly on Google, or turn visitors into calls and quote requests.

  

    
01
Templates aren’t a strategy
A template gives you a starting point. It doesn’t know what your customers need to see, what questions they have, or what makes them trust your business.

    
02
A good website is more than how it looks
The structure, copy, mobile layout, service pages, calls-to-action, contact flow, SEO basics, and trust signals all matter just as much as the design.

    
03
You don’t need another project
You already have a business to run. Hatt Digital handles the structure, design, setup, forms, mobile layout, launch details, and cleanup for you.

    
04
Built to get contacted, not just look nice
A nice-looking site isn’t the goal. The goal is to help people understand your business, trust it, and reach out.
